Quick Start
  1. Go to Pre-processing to generate geometry masks or convert physical units to LBM units.
  2. Go to Solve, pick a simulation type, configure parameters, and click Submit Job.
  3. Monitor progress in the Jobs sidebar (left).
  4. Once complete, go to Post-process to view snapshots, plots, and metadata.
  5. Use Benchmarks to run validation suites against published reference data.

Benchmarks

Marine & Ocean Engineering idle

Cylinder flow (St/Cd), sloshing tank (Faltinsen), near-bed pipeline (St), turbulent channel (log-law), Wigley hull (Cd), SUBOFF resistance (<3% error).

Multiphase LBM idle

Static droplet (Laplace), spinodal decomposition, two-phase Poiseuille comparison (SCMC + CG).

Lid-Driven Cavity – Ghia Comparison idle

Compare centreline velocity profiles against Ghia et al. (1982) reference data at Re = 100 / 400 / 1000.

Single-phase Accuracy Suite idle

Lid-driven cavity (Ghia), backward-facing step (Armaly), and rotating cylinder (Mittal) quantitative validation.

MLUPS Performance idle

D2Q9 BGK throughput in Million Lattice Updates Per Second (MLUPS) at multiple grid sizes.

Porous Media idle

Laplace pressure, capillary invasion, and gas-water drainage benchmarks for porous-media multiphase flow.
