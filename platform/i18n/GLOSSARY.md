## Notes for translators

* Keep mathematical symbols (Re, St, τ, Cd, nx, ny, nz, etc.) in their original Latin/Greek form.
* Unit abbreviations (m, s, m/s, m²/s, lu, ts) are not translated.
* Proper names (Ghia, Faltinsen, Williamson, KRISO, DTMB) are not translated.
* Software names (TensorLBM, FastAPI, Bootstrap) are not translated.
